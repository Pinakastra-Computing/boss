Roles
Business Manager
Performs analysis of the current plans, adds new plans and services. Has full access and read/write rights (except for the system settings and customer profiles editing).
Manager
Approves or disapproves a new customer account after the customer registers in the System. Account Manager approves and assings plans but cannot create/edit/delete a plan or a service. Has partial access and partial read/write rights.
Administrator
Has full access and read/write rights.
Tech support engineer
Performs technical support. Has partial access and only read rights.
